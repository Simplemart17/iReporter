Purpose

iReporter enables citizen to bring any form of corruption to the notice of appropriate authorities and the general public. This platform can also be used to report on things that needs government intervention.

Responsibility

Take initiative and be your brothers' keeper by making a report on how to improve your locality. Let your voice be heard and let people be informed.

Take Action

We are all responsible for the state of our nation, let's build it together
